Koshish

Feel Good  |  Slice of Life  |  Drama

Koshish is a heartwarming story about a father's unwavering support for his daughter after a setback in her exams. Instead of dwelling on her failure, he sees her potential and gently encourages her to rise again. With themes of second chances and perseverance, Koshish reminds us that with the right support, any obstacle can be overcome.
